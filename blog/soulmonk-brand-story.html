Behind the Brand: The Soulmonk Story

By Beyondwill LLP

Streetwear isn’t just about clothes. It’s about community, rebellion, and identity. At the heart of this movement in India is Soulmonk — a brand that blends bold urban expression with sustainability and soul.

The Beginning

Born out of Beyondwill’s vision to incubate purpose-led fashion, Soulmonk was founded to challenge the norms of fast fashion. From sketch to store, the brand has stayed rooted in values: creativity, individuality, and conscience.

What Soulmonk Stands For

More than just streetwear, Soulmonk is a mindset. It’s about dressing the inner monk in the chaos of the urban jungle. It celebrates contradiction — the raw and refined, the spiritual and streetwise.

Design Meets Purpose

Each Soulmonk collection is driven by a theme — sometimes cultural, sometimes political, always personal. Garments feature sustainable fabrics, recycled packaging, and limited-run releases to minimize waste.

Community First

Soulmonk collaborates with local illustrators, underground musicians, and D2C creators. From Instagram drops to pop-up experiences, it doesn’t just sell fashion — it builds subcultures.

The Beyondwill Connection

As part of Beyondwill’s fashion incubator program, Soulmonk received early-stage support in production, funding, mentorship, and brand building. It's a blueprint of how Beyondwill scales independent fashion labels for long-term success.

Where It’s Going

Soulmonk is scaling up — expanding into accessories, deeper collaborations, and storytelling-led campaigns. The mission stays rooted in authenticity and eco-conscious rebellion.
