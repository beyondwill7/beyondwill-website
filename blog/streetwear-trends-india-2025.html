Streetwear Trends in India to Watch in 2025

By Beyondwill LLP

Indian streetwear is leveling up in 2025. Driven by Gen Z, cultural revival, and conscious consumption, the scene is shifting fast. Here are the top trends every creator, curator, and brand should watch out for.

1. Genderless Fashion

Unisex silhouettes are the new normal. Oversized fits, neutral tones, and inclusive messaging are defining collections.

2. Sustainable Materials

Eco-friendly fabrics like hemp, organic cotton, and recycled fibers are dominating drops. Green is the new black.

3. Regional Prints & Art

Designers are remixing Bandhani, Kalamkari, and Madhubani into streetwear palettes — bridging heritage with hype.

4. Influencer-Driven Drops

Limited runs co-designed with local creators, dancers, and musicians give fans deeper emotional connections.

5. NFTs & Phygital Fashion

Some labels are experimenting with digital twins, NFTs, and AR try-ons — blending fashion with future tech.

6. Soulful Storytelling

Brands like Soulmonk stand out by telling stories through each piece — not just what’s trending, but what matters.

Why It’s Bigger Than Style

Streetwear in India isn’t just about what you wear — it’s how you represent where you're from, who you are, and what you believe. The future is expressive, ethical, and Indian at its core.
